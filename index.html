Brock Cooper is a Malibu-based composer creating ambient music to relax, restore, and anchor the nervous system. With a focus on Somatic Listening for expanded states, his work enhances practices like meditation, breathwork, self-development, and psychedelic-assisted therapy.

"Somatic Listening is an exploration of presence—engaging with sound and sensation not as two separate things but as connected parts of the same experience."
